About Yakanashe

Who is Yakanashe?
My name is Ferron, I am a starting software developer who loves making games in Unity, I want to learn more general programming languages, but C# takes the cake in this one. I love science, maths, cosmology, and more! Come and take a look around my Discord server, Linktree , YouTube, Google Play Store , Itch.io , and who knows what the future holds?

Why did I start programming?
I have been interested in discovering how programs worked since I was around 10 years old. Like these pieces of software just work? There had to be more to it, and there was. In grade school, we used simple programs to 'code' our own little projects, and I instantly fell in love. At the end of 2019, I discovered the Unity Game Engine and watched Brackeys and Dani for hours on end. This was very fun, but I didn't learn anything. So I fiddled around with the code until I understood what it did, and after a few years, I understood it and even wrote some of my own code. Now I write almost all code for games myself, and I am still in love with programming!
